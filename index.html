My Work

I always focus on my client's need and one of my goals is to have a fast and efficient turnaround while meeting the standard of the company.

Brigitte Linder

The task is to create a new Optimizepress website for Brigitte Linder that will accept book orders and mentoring, and send out newsletter. I created it using Wordpress with HTML/CSS customization, integrated contact form to Mailchip API for her email marketing platform and connected WooCommerce to Paypal to accepts all kind of orders from the website. After a month of delivery I was so happy that client likes my work and finished ahead of schedule.

Cutting Edge Convention

Client wanted to launch a website for her online summit, this includes sign up or squeeze page, speaker information pages, promotional page, social media infographics, and A/B testing in Google Analytics. Made the landing page with Wordpress using Divi theme with lots of CSS customization. This challenged my CSS skills a lot due to client's limited budget in acquiring Divi paid modules. In which I had to recreate some of these modules and the same time mobile friendly. The time I spent working on her website was worth it because she's super happy and technically I've learned more too.

Ticket To Freedom by Wendy Collier

Client launching her telesummit for the 2nd time. She preferred Wordpress and using Divi and Optimizepress as her themes. My creation involved but not limited to recreation of the landing page's layout, HTML/CSS customization, integration to Aweber (email marketing software) with weekly newsletter setup, speaker interviews editing and infographics for social sharing. Working with her again made it easier because I'm already familiar of the things that needed to be done. I may had encounter url issues on some of her repeated directories but I resolved the matter efficiently.
